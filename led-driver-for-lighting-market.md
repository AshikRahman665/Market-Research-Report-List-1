<p><strong>The LED Driver for Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current LED Drivers</li><li>Constant Voltage LED Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driver market is primarily divided into two types: constant current LED drivers and constant voltage LED drivers. Constant current drivers provide a steady output current, ensuring uniform brightness and performance across LED arrays, making them ideal for applications like street lighting and commercial fixtures. In contrast, constant voltage drivers deliver a fixed voltage to the LEDs, suitable for applications where LED modules operate at a specific voltage level, such as in decorative or architectural lighting.</p></p>
