<p><strong>The Auger Boring Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Ground ABMs</li><li>Hard Rock ABMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auger Boring Machines (ABMs) are categorized into two main types: Soft Ground ABMs and Hard Rock ABMs. Soft Ground ABMs are designed for excavation in moist, cohesive soils such as clay and silt, utilizing a continuous auger system to remove material efficiently. In contrast, Hard Rock ABMs are engineered to tackle challenging geological formations like granite or limestone, equipped with robust cutting heads to penetrate tougher surfaces. Each type is tailored for specific soil conditions, optimizing performance and project outcomes.</p></p>
