The Spin Column Method utilizes silica-based columns to purify nucleic acids through centrifugation, offering simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, the Magnetic Bead Method employs magnetic beads coated with nucleic acid-binding materials, allowing for rapid separation and purification with minimal handling. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnostics and research, enhancing the accuracy of COVID-19 testing and analysis.</p></p>
