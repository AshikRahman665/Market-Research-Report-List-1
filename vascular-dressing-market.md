<p><p>The vascular dressing market is divided into two main types: antimicrobial and non-antimicrobial dressings. Antimicrobial vascular dressings are designed to prevent infections by incorporating agents that inhibit the growth of bacteria, making them ideal for critical wounds. In contrast, non-antimicrobial vascular dressings focus on providing a protective barrier and promoting moisture retention without active antibacterial properties. Both types play crucial roles in wound care, enhancing healing and reducing complications in patients with vascular-related conditions.</p></p>
