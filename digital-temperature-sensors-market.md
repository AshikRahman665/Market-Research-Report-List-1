<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital temperature sensors market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors, such as thermocouples and resistance temperature detectors (RTDs), measure temperature by physically touching the object, providing high accuracy and stability. In contrast, non-contact sensors, like infrared thermometers, detect emitted infrared radiation to gauge temperature from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like automotive, healthcare, and HVAC, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
