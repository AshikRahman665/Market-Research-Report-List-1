Half-bridge gate drivers manage two switches in a half-bridge configuration, useful in inverters. Full bridge gate drivers control four switches for applications like motor drives. Three-phase gate drivers manage three complementary pairs of switches, commonly used in industrial motors. Other drivers encompass specialized configurations for unique requirements, catering to diverse sectors in power electronics.</p></p>
