<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switches</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual waveguide switches market primarily features two types: rectangular waveguide switches and double ridge waveguide switches. Rectangular waveguide switches are designed for standard waveguide sizes and provide efficient signal control with minimal loss. In contrast, double ridge waveguide switches offer enhanced bandwidth and improved performance, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications and radar systems, facilitating effective signal routing and ensuring reliable performance in complex waveguide networks.</p></p>
