<p><p>The palm recognition biometric system market encompasses two primary types: palmprint recognition and palm vein recognition. Palmprint recognition analyzes the unique patterns of ridges and lines on the palm's surface, used for identification and authentication applications. In contrast, palm vein recognition focuses on the unique vascular patterns beneath the skin, offering a higher level of security due to its difficulty to replicate. Both methods are employed in sectors such as banking, healthcare, and security, providing reliable identity verification solutions.</p></p>
