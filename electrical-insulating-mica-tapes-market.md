<p><p>The electrical insulating mica tapes market consists primarily of mica glass and mica polyester tapes. Mica glass tape combines mica with a glass fabric base, offering excellent thermal resistance and mechanical strength, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. Mica polyester tape, on the other hand, uses a polyester backing, providing flexibility and lightweight properties while still retaining good insulation and temperature resistance. Both types are crucial in electrical insulation, enhancing performance and safety in various industrial applications.</p></p>
