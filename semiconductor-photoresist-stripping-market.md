<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Stripping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripping</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist stripping market is primarily categorized into positive and negative photoresist stripping. Positive photoresist stripping involves the removal of positive photoresist materials, where exposure to light transforms the resist into a soluble form for easy removal, enabling precise pattern definition on substrates. Conversely, negative photoresist stripping targets negative photoresists, where exposed areas become insoluble, making them more challenging to remove. Each type addresses specific manufacturing needs in the semiconductor fabrication process, impacting overall efficiency and yield.</p></p>
