<p><p>Ostomy care products primarily consist of one-piece and two-piece bag systems, designed for individuals with ostomies. One-piece bags integrate the adhesive skin barrier and pouch, offering convenience and a lower profile, making them suitable for active users. In contrast, two-piece bags separate the skin barrier and pouch, allowing for easier pouch changes while maintaining the same barrier. This flexibility can be beneficial for users with sensitive skin or those requiring frequent changes, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
