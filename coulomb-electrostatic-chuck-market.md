<p><strong>The Coulomb Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Multipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coulomb Electrostatic Chuck market is primarily divided into two types: unipolar and multipolar. Unipolar electrostatic chucks utilize a single electrode to generate an electrostatic field, effectively holding wafers in place during processing. In contrast, multipolar electrostatic chucks employ multiple electrodes, allowing for more uniform electrostatic force distribution and improved wafer clamping stability. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, with applications varying based on processing requirements and desired precision in wafer handling.</p></p>
