<p><p>The Sulfur Recovery Units (SRUs) market is primarily categorized into three types: Claus Process, Tail Gas Treatment Units (TGTUs), and Others. The Claus Process is the most common method, converting hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur. Tail Gas Treatment focuses on refining the by-products from the Claus Process to enhance sulfur recovery and minimize emissions. The "Others" category encompasses alternative technologies and innovations in sulfur recovery, such as bioprocesses and advanced catalytic systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
