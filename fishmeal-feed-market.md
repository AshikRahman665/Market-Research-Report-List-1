<p><strong>The Fishmeal Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishmeal feed market primarily comprises two types: Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD) fishmeal. SD fishmeal is produced using steam and high pressure, ensuring higher protein retention and lower oxidation, making it suitable for aquaculture feeds. Conversely, FD fishmeal employs direct flames for drying, resulting in a distinct flavor and nutritional profile, typically preferred in animal feeds. Each type serves specific dietary needs in livestock and aquaculture, influencing product quality and market demand.</p></p>
