<p><p>The venous indwelling needles market is categorized into two main types: open indwelling needles and closed indwelling needles. Open indwelling needles allow easy access to the venous system and are typically used for short-term procedures, promoting rapid drug delivery. Closed indwelling needles, on the other hand, feature a sealed design that minimizes infection risk and is ideal for long-term use, such as in patients requiring extended intravenous therapy. Both types address varying clinical needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
