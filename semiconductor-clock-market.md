<p><ul><li>Real Time Clock(RTC)</li><li>Semiconductor Clock IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor clock market primarily comprises two key segments: Real Time Clocks (RTC) and semiconductor clock ICs. RTCs are specialized devices designed to keep accurate time even when the main power supply is off, often utilizing a battery backup. They are essential in applications like computers, embedded systems, and consumer electronics. In contrast, semiconductor clock ICs generate precise clock signals for timing-related functions in various electronic devices, ensuring synchronization across circuits and improving performance in digital applications.</p></p>
