<p><p>The semi-automatic filling machine market is categorized into three main types: volumetric, mass, and others. Volumetric filling machines dispense products based on specific volume measurements, ideal for liquids and granular materials. Mass filling machines, on the other hand, measure the weight of the product to ensure accuracy during filling, making them suitable for dense or highly variable materials. The "others" category encompasses various specialized filling equipment tailored to unique applications across industries, enhancing versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
