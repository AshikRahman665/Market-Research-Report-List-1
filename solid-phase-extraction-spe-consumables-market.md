<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) consumables market comprises three primary types: cartridges, well plates, and disks. Cartridges are cylindrical devices filled with sorbent material, suitable for various sample volumes. Well plates, designed for high-throughput applications, allow simultaneous processing of multiple samples, enhancing efficiency. Disks are flat materials used for specific applications, providing faster flow rates and lower solvent volumes. Together, these types cater to diverse analytical needs, facilitating the extraction and purification of compounds from complex matrices.</p></p>
