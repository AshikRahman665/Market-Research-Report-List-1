<p><p>The multilayer thick film circuit substrate market includes two main types: Alumina substrates and Aluminum Nitride (AlN) substrates. Alumina substrates are widely used due to their excellent dielectric properties, thermal stability, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various electronic applications. AlN substrates, on the other hand, offer superior thermal conductivity and are preferred in high-power or high-frequency applications, enhancing performance in industries such as telecommunications and automotive. Both types cater to the growing demand for reliable and efficient electronic devices.</p></p>
