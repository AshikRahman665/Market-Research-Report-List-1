<p><ul><li>BSF Solar Cells</li><li>PERC Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV metallization aluminum paste market is primarily segmented into three types based on solar cell technologies: BSF (Back-Contact Solar Cells), PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) solar cells, and conventional cells. BSF solar cells utilize aluminum paste to create rear back-surface fields, enhancing efficiency by reflecting unabsorbed light. PERC solar cells incorporate aluminum paste for a passivated layer that improves performance and lowers recombination losses. Both types leverage advanced metallization for optimal energy conversion in photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
