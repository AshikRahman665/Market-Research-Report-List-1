The Curve Part allows for smooth transitions and navigational flexibility, essential for maintaining flow in various settings. The 360 Degree Loop Part introduces a circular design, enabling complete rotation within confined spaces. Together, these components enhance functionality in transportation systems, amusement parks, and industrial applications.</p></p>
