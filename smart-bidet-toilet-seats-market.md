<p><p>The smart bidet toilet seats market features two primary heating technologies: storage heating and instantaneous heating. Storage heating systems warm water using a tank, providing a readily available supply, but may be limited by tank capacity and heat recovery time. In contrast, instantaneous heating systems generate hot water on-demand, ensuring a continuous supply without waiting. This flexibility caters to diverse consumer preferences, with storage heating offering steady warmth, while instantaneous heating promotes efficiency and space-saving designs, making each type suitable for various bathroom environments.</p></p>
