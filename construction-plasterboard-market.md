Regular plasterboard is commonly used for standard interior walls and ceilings, providing a smooth finish for painting. Moisture Resistant plasterboard, often used in areas like bathrooms and kitchens, features a special coating to withstand humidity and prevent mold. Fire Resistant plasterboard is designed to enhance fire safety, incorporating materials that impede the spread of flames, making it ideal for high-risk areas like commercial buildings and corridors.</p></p>
