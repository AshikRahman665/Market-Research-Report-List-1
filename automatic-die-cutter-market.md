<p><strong>The Automatic Die-Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Die Cutter</li><li>Flat Bed Die Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic die-cutter market primarily features two types: rotary die cutters and flatbed die cutters. Rotary die cutters utilize cylindrical dies to produce continuous patterns, making them ideal for high-speed production of intricate designs on various materials, such as paper and film. In contrast, flatbed die cutters employ a flat die on a stationary bed, suitable for precise cuts on thicker materials and enabling complex shapes. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in packaging, labeling, and product manufacturing processes.</p></p>
