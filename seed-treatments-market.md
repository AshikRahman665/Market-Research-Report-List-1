<p><p>The seed treatments market is divided into two primary types: chemical and non-chemical seed treatments. Conversely, non-chemical seed treatments utilize natural methods, such as biological agents, physical processes, or organic coatings, to safeguard seeds from diseases and pests without synthetic chemicals. Both approaches aim to improve crop yield and resilience, catering to diverse agricultural practices.</p></p>
