<p><strong>The Resistance Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quarter-bridge</li><li>Half-bridge</li><li>Full-bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance bridge market types include quarter-bridge, half-bridge, and full-bridge configurations, each with distinct applications in measuring resistance values. A quarter-bridge utilizes one active gauge and three passive resistors, ideal for strain measurements. The half-bridge employs two active gauges, enhancing sensitivity and temperature compensation, suitable for dynamic measurements. The full-bridge configuration features four active gauges, providing maximum accuracy and balanced output, often used in precise load and pressure sensing applications, making it the most versatile option for complex measurement tasks.</p></p>
