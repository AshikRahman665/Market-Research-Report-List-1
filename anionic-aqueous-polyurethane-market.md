<p><p>The anionic aqueous polyurethane market encompasses two main types: carboxylic acid type and sulfonic acid type polyurethanes. Carboxylic acid type polyurethanes feature carboxylic functional groups that enhance dispersion and stability in water-based applications, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, sulfonic acid type polyurethanes contain sulfonic groups, which provide excellent hydrophilicity and increase adhesion properties, ideal for textile and automotive industries. Both types are valued for their eco-friendliness and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
