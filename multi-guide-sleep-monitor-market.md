<p><p>The Multi Guide Sleep Monitor market consists of three primary types: EGG Monitors, Dynamic Monitors, and Video Monitors. EGG Monitors utilize electroencephalography to assess brain activity during sleep, providing insights into sleep stages. Dynamic Monitors track physiological changes such as heart rate and movement, offering a comprehensive view of sleep patterns. Video Monitors capture visual data of the sleeper, allowing for analysis of behaviors and disturbances. Together, these technologies enhance understanding of sleep quality and disorders.</p></p>
