<p><p>Fluorosilicone compounds are versatile materials categorized into three main market types: liquid, solid, and semisolid. Liquid fluorosilicones are used for coatings and adhesives due to their exceptional flexibility and resistance to various chemicals. Solid fluorosilicones offer robust mechanical properties and are ideal for applications requiring durability and thermal stability, such as gaskets and seals. Semisolid fluorosilicones bridge the gap, providing a balance of viscosity and performance, making them suitable for lubricants and potting compounds in electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
