<p><p>The MEMS flow sensor market consists of two primary types: MEMS gas flow sensors and MEMS liquid flow sensors. MEMS gas flow sensors detect and measure the flow of gases, utilizing micro-electromechanical systems for enhanced sensitivity and accuracy. Applications include HVAC, automotive, and environmental monitoring. Conversely, MEMS liquid flow sensors measure the flow of liquids, commonly used in medical devices, industrial processes, and automotive applications. Both types are compact, energy-efficient, and increasingly essential for precise flow management in various industries.</p></p>
