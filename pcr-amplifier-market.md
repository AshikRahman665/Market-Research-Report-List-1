<p><p>The PCR amplifier market encompasses various types suited for specific applications. Ordinary PCR amplifiers are standard devices for basic DNA amplification. Gradient PCR amplifiers allow temperature variations across the block for optimal annealing. In situ PCR amplifiers facilitate amplification directly within cells or tissues, preserving morphological context. Real-time fluorescence quantification PCR amplifiers enable monitoring of DNA amplification in real-time, providing quantitative data with high sensitivity. Each type caters to distinct research and clinical needs, enhancing molecular biology capabilities.</p></p>
