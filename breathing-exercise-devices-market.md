Two-flow devices provide two options for airflow, making them versatile for various breathing exercises. Single-flow devices focus on one airflow pathway, ideal for targeted breathing techniques and easier for beginner users. Each type serves specific therapeutic needs for improved lung function and overall respiratory health.</p></p>
