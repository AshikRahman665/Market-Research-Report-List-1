<p><p>The Low Density Lipoprotein (LDL) Test Kit market comprises three main types: LDL-C, LDL-P, and LDL-B. LDL-C type measures the cholesterol content in LDL particles, providing insights into cardiovascular risk. LDL-P type quantifies the actual number of LDL particles, offering a more comprehensive view of atherosclerotic risk. LDL-B type assesses the size and density of LDL particles, with smaller, denser particles being more atherogenic. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes in evaluating heart health and disease risk.</p></p>
