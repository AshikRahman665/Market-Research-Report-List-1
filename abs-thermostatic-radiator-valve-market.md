<p><p>The ABS thermostatic radiator valve market features two primary types: self-operated temperature control valves and electric temperature control valves. Self-operated valves utilize a mechanical mechanism to automatically regulate water flow based on room temperature, ensuring energy efficiency without requiring external power. In contrast, electric temperature control valves rely on electronic components to precisely manage heating, often allowing for advanced programming and remote control. Both types enhance comfort and energy efficiency in heating systems, catering to different consumer preferences and technological needs.</p></p>
