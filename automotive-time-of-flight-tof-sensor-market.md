<p><p>The Automotive Time of Flight (ToF) Sensor market is divided into two main types: Direct ToF Sensors and Indirect ToF Sensors. Direct ToF Sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, providing precise distance measurements, ideal for applications like obstacle detection. Indirect ToF Sensors, on the other hand, utilize phase shift techniques to estimate distance, typically offering higher accuracy over longer ranges, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicle applications.</p></p>
