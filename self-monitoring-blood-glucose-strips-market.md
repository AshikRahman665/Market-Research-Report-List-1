<p><p>Self-monitoring blood glucose strips are categorized based on the enzymatic methods used for glucose measurement. Glucose oxidase strips utilize the enzyme glucose oxidase to facilitate a reaction that produces a measurable electrical signal, widely recognized for their reliability. Glucose dehydrogenase strips employ glucose dehydrogenase, offering improved accuracy with various blood glucose levels and potentially less sensitivity to interference. Other market types may include alternative technologies, such as optical or electrochemical methods, which provide diverse options for glucose monitoring.</p></p>
