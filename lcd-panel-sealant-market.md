<p><p>The LCD panel sealant market is divided into high viscosity and low viscosity types based on their application and properties. High viscosity sealants provide a thicker, more durable bond, making them ideal for applications requiring strong adhesion and resistance to environmental factors. In contrast, low viscosity sealants flow easily into small gaps, ensuring a better seal and efficient application. Both types are crucial for the manufacturing of LCD panels, catering to different needs in terms of bonding strength and application precision.</p></p>
