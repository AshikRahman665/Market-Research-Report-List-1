<p><p>The SCR water control valves market is categorized primarily into two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves regulate flow in a single direction, providing simple on/off control, making them ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, 3-way valves can either mix or divert flow between multiple pipelines, offering greater flexibility and control in diverse systems. These valves are essential in various industries, including water management, HVAC, and industrial processes, facilitating efficient fluid regulation and system performance.</p></p>
