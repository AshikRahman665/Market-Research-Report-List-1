<p><p>The Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) device market is divided into two main types: implantable and non-implantable devices. Non-implantable SCS devices, on the other hand, are temporary and can be used for shorter durations, usually through external electrodes or leads, making them suitable for trial purposes or patients looking for less invasive options.</p></p>
