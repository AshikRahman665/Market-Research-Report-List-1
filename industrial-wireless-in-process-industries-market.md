<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless in process industries encompasses various market types tailored for different operational needs. Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) facilitates high-speed data communication over shorter distances within facilities. Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN) connects devices over limited ranges, ideal for small-scale applications. Satellite (GNSS) offers global positioning and tracking, enhancing remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) serves long-range communications with minimal power consumption, suitable for IoT devices. Cellular technologies provide robust coverage, supporting mobile connectivity across extensive industrial operations.</p></p>
